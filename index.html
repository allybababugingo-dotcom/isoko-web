Message From Our Founder

Eric MUNYANEZA is the Founder and Managing Director of Isoko y’Umuco Ltd, a cultural and arts-based social enterprise dedicated to advancing education and improving the welfare of underprivileged children in Rwanda and the wider region. He is driven by the core conviction that every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, deserves an equal opportunity to access education, nurture their talents, and build a hopeful future.

Under his leadership, Isoko y’Umuco Ltd serves as a transformative platform that utilizes arts and culture as a catalyst for social reintegration. The organization specifically supports children facing hardships—including poverty, disability, and orphanage—helping them return to school and regain their footing in society. By leveraging creative disciplines such as traditional dance, poetry, theater, and music, Eric believes that a child’s natural talent can become a sustainable fountain of development and opportunity.

Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in Rwanda and the region, fostering a society where every child—regardless of their financial means—has the opportunity to learn, develop their talents, and live with the hope of a bright future through the power of culture and the arts.

Mission

To reintegrate underprivileged children into the education system and empower them psychologically and professionally through artistic discovery, school supply funding, and instilling self-confidence for successful social integration.

Key Objectives

Educational Retention

Reducing school dropout rates caused by poverty.

Talent Development

Professionalizing abilities for financial independence.

Psychosocial Support

Using arts as a therapeutic tool for healing and trauma.
